## Project Overview

Waffle Zoa is a local restaurant based in Ga-Sekororo, Limpopo, founded in 2021 by Chef Thabo Semelani. The restaurant specialises in a unique fusion menu including waffle burgers, chicken wings, pizza, seafood, and cocktails. Chef Thabo studied overseas in Japan, where he learned about bold flavours, food presentation, and the power of food to bring people together.

This website project aims to create a professional, mobile-responsive, and user-friendly website for Waffle Zoa that will showcase the full menu, enable online ordering, allow table reservations, and provide customers with an engaging digital experience.

### Mission statement:
Waffle Zoa is here to serve fresh, flavourful food with a smile. We bring waffle burgers, wings, pizza, seafood, and cocktails to Ga-Sekororo, Limpopo, because we believe you shouldn't have to choose. Every customer who walks in becomes family. We cook with passion and serve with heart.

### Vision statement:
We want to become the most loved local restaurant in Limpopo – the place where neighbours become regulars. As we grow, we promise to keep our small-town soul. Above all, we want to keep Chef Thabo's dream alive, one plate at a time.

## website goals and objectives

- Increase online orders for takeaway and delivery
- Generate leads for event bookings and large-group reservations
- Provide clear, accessible menu information
- Reduce manual workload by automating reservations and order processing
- Showcase Waffle Zoa's unique fusion concept (waffle burgers, wings, pizza, seafood, cocktails)

### Key performance indicator(KPI)
•	Monthly online order volume
•	Monthly online order revenue (ZAR)
•	Average order value (AOV)
•	Conversion rate (visitors to completed orders)
•	Percentage of total sales from online channel

## Design and user experience

 ### Colour schemes
The proposed colour scheme for Waffle Zoa is based on a carefully selected palette from Color Hunt, an online platform that provides curated four-colour combinations for design projects (Color Hunt, 2026). Warm yellow (#F5A623) stimulates appetite and energy, deep blue (#1E3A5F) conveys trust and professionalism, coral red (#E5533D) creates urgency for call-to-action buttons, while white (#FFFFFF) and charcoal grey (#333333) ensure readability and a clean layout (ColorHunt,2026).

### Typography
We'll keep things simple and readable by using Calibri for the entire Waffle Zoa website. Calibri is clean, warm, and easy to read on both phones and computers (Microsoft,2021). 
Headings and buttons will use Calibri Bold to stand out, while body text and menu descriptions will use Calibri Regular for a comfortable reading experience.

### Layout and design approach 
•	Design style: Modern, vibrant, and uncluttered – reflecting a lively local restaurant.
•	Homepage layout: Hero banner with waffle burger image → quick-order buttons → menu category grid → customer favourites → location and hours 
•	Menu page: Filterable by category  to reduce scrolling.

### User experience considerations 
•	Navigation: Simple top bar with 5–6 items. “Order Online” button highlighted.
•	Clear calls-to-action: Every page should lead to either “Order” or “Book a Table”
