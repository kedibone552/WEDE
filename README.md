## Project Overview

Waffle Zoa is a local restaurant based in Ga-Sekororo, Limpopo, founded in 2021 by Chef Thabo Semelani. The restaurant specialises in a unique fusion menu including waffle burgers, chicken wings, pizza, seafood, and cocktails. Chef Thabo studied overseas in Japan, where he learned about bold flavours, food presentation, and the power of food to bring people together.

This website project aims to create a professional, mobile-responsive, and user-friendly website for Waffle Zoa that will showcase the full menu, enable online ordering, allow table reservations, and provide customers with an engaging digital experience.

## website goals and objectives

- Increase online orders for takeaway and delivery
- Generate leads for event bookings and large-group reservations
- Provide clear, accessible menu information
- Reduce manual workload by automating reservations and order processing
- Showcase Waffle Zoa's unique fusion concept (waffle burgers, wings, pizza, seafood, cocktails)
